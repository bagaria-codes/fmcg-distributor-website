About Prime Traders

Your trusted partner in FMCG distribution across India.

Our Story

Prime Traders embarked on its journey in 2005 with a clear vision: to revolutionize the FMCG distribution landscape in India. Starting from humble beginnings, we have grown into a leading wholesale distributor, connecting top brands with a vast network of retailers across the nation.

Our commitment to efficiency, reliability, and unparalleled customer satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our success. We pride ourselves on understanding the dynamic needs of the Indian market and delivering solutions that foster mutual growth for our brand partners and clients.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To be the most trusted and efficient FMCG distribution partner, enabling seamless access to quality products for every household in India, while driving sustainable growth for our brand partners and retailers.

Our Vision

To redefine excellence in distribution, leveraging cutting-edge technology and a dedicated workforce to create unparalleled value for our stakeholders and contribute positively to the economic development of the regions we serve.

Our Core Values

Integrity

Upholding the highest ethical standards in all our dealings, ensuring transparency and trust with every interaction.

Innovation

Continuously seeking new and better ways to serve our clients and optimize our supply chain for maximum efficiency.

Customer Focus

Placing our clients' needs at the heart of our operations, striving for their success as our top priority.

Growth

Committed to sustained growth for our company, our partners, and the communities we operate in.

Reliability

Delivering on our promises consistently, ensuring timely and accurate distribution services every time.

Sustainability

Operating responsibly to minimize environmental impact and contribute to a healthier planet.
